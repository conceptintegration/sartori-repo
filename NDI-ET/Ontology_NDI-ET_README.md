# National Democratic Institute - Global Lessons on Managing Executive Transitions (NDI-ET)

- **Description**: The NDI Executive Transitions Glossary covers key terms related to executive transition, the transfer of power from one leader and administration to another. The glossary provides an overview of these various processes from the perspective of different political systems and contexts--presidential, parliamentary, semi-presidential, and post-conflict.
